Welcome to CentralETEC

You're about to find the app that will make your journey to your ETEC easier and informative than ever. Our application is more than a navigation assistant, it is a complete educational companion for you. Explore our website to discover the incredible functionality to identify safe routes, help via chatbot and to obtain information about the educational institution.

How the app works

• Enter your destination:
Just enter your destination and our app will do the rest. It will automatically calculate the safest route with multiple parameters in mind.
• Alerts and Updates:
Receive real-time alerts about traffic, areas to avoid and other important information for a safe journey.
• Share with the community:
Be part of a community where everyone's safety is a priority.
• Questions answered by the Chatbot:
Start a conversation with our Chatbot to get instant answers about the institution.
